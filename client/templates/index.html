FREQUENCY QUESTION


The BeeBox App is a goods transport and logistics mobile application designed to offer: Fast, Safe, and Easy booking of freight and cargo services.

You can use the BeeBox App to quickly and safely book goods transport services right from your phone. Whether you're a business owner or an individual needing to move packages, the app makes the process simple. First, you open the BeeBox App and select your pickup and delivery city or branch. Then, you enter basic details like the type of goods, weight, and delivery preferences. The app will instantly show you the estimated cost and time. Once you confirm the booking, a nearby vehicle is assigned, and you can track its location in real time. You'll also get updates through notifications, and after delivery, you can view or download proof of delivery. With its clean interface, fast service, and focus on safety, BeeBox makes transporting goods as easy as ordering a cab.

To book a tempo, mini truck, bike delivery, or intercity courier service from the BeeBox App, simply open the app and start by selecting the type of vehicle or service you need based on your package size and distance. Choose your pickup location and delivery destination, whether it's within the city or to another city. Then, enter the details of the goods—such as weight, quantity, and any special instructions. The app will show you an instant fare estimate and available vehicles nearby. Once you confirm the booking, BeeBox assigns a verified driver or rider to pick up your items. You can then track your shipment in real time, receive status updates, and get digital proof of delivery upon completion. The entire process is designed to be fast, reliable, and completely contactless, making BeeBox the smart choice for safe and efficient goods transport.

🚚 Yes, Bee Box Offers Inter-City Transportation Services!

Whether you're moving goods from one city to another or seeking courier pickups along the way, Bee Box is designed to handle it efficiently. Here’s how it works:

  1. Select Inter-City Option: In the app, choose the “Inter-City” service for bookings crossing city or state lines.
  2. Enter Pickup & Delivery Cities: Pick your origin and destination from the list of Bee Box coverage areas.
  3. Provide Shipment Details: Specify your package type (tempo, mini-truck, bike courier), weight, dimensions, and any special requirements.
  4. Instant Estimate & Booking: You’ll receive fare and delivery-time estimates instantly. Once you confirm, the system assigns a verified vehicle and driver from the relevant network.
  5. Real-Time Tracking & Notifications: Track your shipment in real time. You'll also get SMS/app notifications at key stages—pickup, in transit, and delivery.
  6. Proof of Delivery: On arrival, you’ll get digital proof—an e-signature or photo confirmation.

So yes, Bee Box simplifies safe, speedy, and hassle‑free inter‑city transportation— just like it does for local deliveries.

❌ Prohibited Items

Bee Box doesn’t transport items that are illegal, hazardous, or present safety risks. These include:

  • Dangerous goods & chemicals: Explosives, flammable liquids/solids (paints, thinners, aerosols), corrosives, oxidizers, pesticides, and radioactive or toxic substances.
  • Lithium and large batteries, fuel-filled machinery or vehicles, pressurized containers (gas cylinders, aerosol cans).
  • Live animals or remains, human remains, biological samples, and infectious material.
  • Currency & negotiable financial items: Cash, cheques, traveler’s cheques, bonds, credit cards.
  • Precious metals & stones: Jewelry, bullion, and valuable artwork.
  • Firearms, ammunition, explosives, weapon replicas.
  • Alcohol, tobacco, narcotics, and controlled substances.
  • Perishable food, plants, seeds, and non-vegetarian items.
  • Electronics & battery-powered devices in certain services (e.g., international shipments).

📝 Why These Restrictions?

  • Safety: Prevents fire, chemical spills, or contamination.
  • Legal compliance: Aligns with national and international transport laws.
  • Item integrity: Avoids damage to other shipments or the courier network.

✅ What You Can Do Instead

  • Ask for specialized logistics: Some items require special handling (e.g., hazardous materials, electronics).
  • Contact Bee Box support: They can guide you on permitted packaging or alternative transport methods.

Bee Box - Types of Goods Transported

Bee Box is a logistics platform that specializes in facilitating the transportation of various types of goods across India. While it doesn't offer traditional packers and movers services, it provides flexible and on-demand transportation solutions for a wide range of items.

Types of Goods Transported by Bee Box:

  • Household Items: Bee Box can assist in transporting household goods, such as furniture, appliances, and personal belongings, especially for intercity moves.
  • Office Equipment: Businesses can use Bee Box to move office furniture, electronics, and other equipment during relocations or branch setups.
  • Commercial Goods: Retailers and wholesalers can transport inventory, stock, and supplies between locations.
  • E-commerce Shipments: Bee Box supports the movement of e-commerce parcels, ensuring timely deliveries to customers.
  • Industrial Equipment: The platform can handle the transportation of machinery and industrial tools, though for very heavy or oversized items, specialized services might be required.
  • Perishable Goods: While Bee Box primarily focuses on general cargo, it may accommodate perishable items with appropriate packaging and handling instructions.

Service Features:

  • On-Demand Bookings: Users can book transportation services as needed, providing flexibility for both businesses and individuals.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Bee Box offers tracking capabilities, allowing customers to monitor their shipments throughout the journey.
  • Multiple Vehicle Options: Depending on the size and nature of the goods, Bee Box provides various vehicle options, including mini trucks and two-wheelers, to suit different transportation needs.

For specific requirements or to confirm if Bee Box can handle particular types of goods, it's advisable to contact their customer support directly. They can provide detailed information and assist in planning the most suitable transportation solution for your needs.

The transit time for transporting goods with Bee Box varies based on several factors, including distance, vehicle type, and the nature of the shipment. Here's a general overview:

  • Local Deliveries (Within the Same City): Typically completed within a few hours to the same day, depending on traffic and distance.
  • Intercity Transport: Delivery times usually range from 1 to 3 days for most major cities, depending on the distance between pickup and delivery locations.
  • Urgent or Express Shipments: Bee Box offers options for faster deliveries at additional cost, with some shipments arriving on the same day or within 24 hours.

Real-Time Tracking allows customers to monitor their shipment status throughout the journey, providing transparency and updates on expected delivery times.

API Integration with BeeBox

API integration with BeeBox allows your application or system to connect directly with BeeBox’s logistics platform for seamless management of transportation services. By integrating BeeBox’s API, you can automate booking requests, track shipments in real-time, manage invoices, and receive status updates without manual intervention.

Typically, you start by obtaining API credentials (like an API key) from BeeBox after registration. Your system then sends structured requests (usually in JSON format) to BeeBox’s endpoints to create bookings, fetch vehicle availability, or get tracking information. BeeBox processes these requests and returns responses that your system can use to update your interface or trigger further actions.

This integration ensures faster processing, reduces errors, and enhances the customer experience by providing up-to-date shipment data directly within your own application or website.

For logistics and transportation, APIs allow instant booking, tracking, and status updates, improving customer satisfaction and operational efficiency. It also enables scalability, as businesses can easily add new services or partners without overhauling existing systems.

Ultimately, API integration streamlines operations, cuts costs, and enhances decision-making by providing timely and reliable data.
